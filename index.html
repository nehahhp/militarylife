Drop choices on the chart. Drag an existing choice icon left or right to move it to a different age. Move your mouse or touch the graph to see dollar amounts. Purple investment balance uses the right-side axis and shows accumulated savings plus investment growth at 8% per year. It does not include home equity, car value, pension value, or other assets. College, car, child, and emergency costs now end after their default durations. Military Separation / Retirement shows what happens when active-duty service ends before or after 20 years of service.
